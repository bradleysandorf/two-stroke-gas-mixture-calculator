Two Stroke Gasoline Mixture Calculator

Two stroke engines, commonly found in chainsaws, leaf blowers, outboard engines, pressure washers, and other smaller engines require a specific mixture of gasoline and oil to run properly. Failing to add the proper amount of oil to your gasoline/ petrol mixture can cause serious damage to your engine, as two stroke engines are lubricated by the oil in their gas/ oil mixture. This calculator can help you determine the correct amount of oil that you need to add to your gasoline to ensure that the proper ratio of gasoline to oil is achieved. Typically, you can find the ratio needed for your two stroke engine in your owner's manual or printed on the side of the engine.
